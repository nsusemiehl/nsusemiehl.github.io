About Me

I am a rising junion at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor. I am majoring in Astronomy & Astrophysics and plan to minor in Physics and Statistics. My research interests include exoplanet detection, star and planet formation, and big data. I am currently working with Professor Michael Meyer at UofM researching the companion frequency of M-dwarf stars. I also have a passion for computer programming and a growing interests in data science and web development. I am proficient in Python, and I have experience in C++, HTML, CSS, and MySQL.
