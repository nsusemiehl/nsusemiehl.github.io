About Me

I am a junior at the University of Michigan - Ann Arbor majoring in Astronomy & Astrophysics and minoring in Physics and Statistics. I am passionate about the universe and our place in it. My research interests include exoplanet detection, star and planet formation, and data analysis. I am currently working with Professor Michael Meyer at UofM researching the companion frequency of M-dwarf stars. I am excited to pursue a career in space science, whether that be in the data science industry or academia.
